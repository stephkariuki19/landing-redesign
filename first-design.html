About Us

KeNVIP is a repository with a dashboard for supporting decision making at the NVIP and its partners. The repository connects together and preserves both historical and contemporary data that belong to NVIP providing a central cloud services for accessing, analyzing, visualizing and sharing data. It also serves as a resource for training and operational activities of NVIP. KeNVIP will be available for use by authorized persons in accordance to their respective roles as defined by NVIP.
